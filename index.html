ABOUT US

Digital Innovation
and Technology Development Center

The Emare ICT Hub Center is designed to become a leading platform for digital skills development, technology entrepreneurship, and innovation in Debre Birehan City. This center aims to provide accessible digital tools, training, mentorship, and collaboration opportunities to local tech professionals, startups, and businesses With the growing importance of technology in driving economic development, the Emare ICT Hub will be a vital player in fostering a tech-savvy community, empowering young innovators, and creating job opportunities through digital literacy
